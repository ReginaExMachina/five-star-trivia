## [I. OPERATOR'S INTRODUCTION]

**FIVE STAR TRIVIA** is an arcade-inspired challenge for film students and casual moviegoers alike. We have optimized this interface for maximum nostalgic output, using 8-bit styling and high-contrast visuals.

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is simple: Navigate a century of film history, maintain your streak, and earn a perfect **5-Star Rating** from the elusive "Critic’s Circle" (the Game Engine).

---

## [II. GAME MODES & CONFIGURATION]

*Attention: Use the left-hand menu to configure your experience before starting the round.*

[ ]   **THE ERA SELECTION**
      Configure the game engine to pull from specific time periods, or select "All" for a true global tour of cinema.
      * *SILENT ERA:* (e.g., *Metropolis*, *Nosferatu*)
      * *GOLDEN AGE:* (e.g., *The Wizard of Oz*, Hitchcock)
      * *NEW HOLLYWOOD:* (e.g., *Godfather*, *Taxi Driver*)
      * *THE 80s/90s:* (e.g., *Jaws*, *Top Gun*, *Clueless*)
      * *MODERN:* (e.g., *Parasite*, *The Avengers*, *Barbie*)

[ ]   **MARKET SELECTION (REGION)**
      * *[X] DOMESTIC:* Focus on Hollywood-centric questions.
      * *[ ] INTERNATIONAL:* Expand your focus to World Cinema.

## [III. DIFFICULTY CALIBRATION]

Choose your level of expert analysis. If your choice has fewer than 5 valid questions, the Game Engine will backfill (automatically fill) from the wider question pool.

      | LEVEL | DESCRIPTION | STAKES |
      |---|---|---|
      | **EASY** | Casual Moviegoer | Low. Safe for newcomers. |
      | **MEDIUM** | The Weekend Marathoner | Normal. Some deep cuts. |
      | **HARD** | Student of Film | High. Focus on DP, Director, Year. |
      | **DIRECTOR'S CUT** | The Film Scholar | EXTREME. |

## [IV. CONTROLS & OBJECTIVE]

[START] LIGHTS, CAMERA, ACTION!
        Press to begin your round of 5 questions.

[SELECT] The Left Menu (Era, Region, Difficulty)
        Toggle these settings to customize your "script."

[A/B/C] TRIVIA ANSWERS
        Click the box corresponding to the correct film fact.

**YOUR OBJECTIVE:**
Achieve a **5-Star Score** (5 correct answers) in your chosen configuration. Maintain your streak to unlock special "critic" bonuses (e.g., personal glory).
